How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address, and a password reset link will be sent to your email.

What should I do if I cannot log in to my account?
If you are unable to log in, please check if your email and password are correct. If the issue persists, reset your password or contact our support team.

How can I update my profile information?
To update your profile information, log in to your account, go to the "Profile" section, and click "Edit." Make the necessary changes and click "Save."

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments via credit card, debit card, PayPal, and bank transfers. Please note that available options may vary by region.

Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Log in to your account, navigate to the "Subscriptions" page, and click "Cancel Subscription."

How do I contact customer support?
You can contact customer support by emailing support@company.com or by calling our hotline at +1-800-123-4567.

Is there a way to track my order?
Yes, you can track your order by logging in to your account and clicking on the "Orders" section. There, you will find the tracking information for your shipment.

What is your refund policy?
Our refund policy allows you to request a refund within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to the "Refund Policy" section on our website for detailed terms and conditions.
